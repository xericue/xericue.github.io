xericuan axioms

these are axioms i've formulated regarding my experience across the years.

  • invest in the three pillars of health - sleep, water intake, and exercise
  • love what you do, and love yourself when you cannot love what you do
  • be delusional about what you can control and pragmatic about what you can't
  • you are human and therefore capable of love and change
  • complacency destroys the soul - if something doesn't challenge you, it doesn't change you
  • everything is learnable - if your environment is holding you back, move
  • do something just for yourself, little by little every day
  • do something just for your future, little by little every day
  • stay consistent - build the skills in the fields into which you want to grow
  • stay inconsistent - if something isnt working, take note of it, and do something differently
